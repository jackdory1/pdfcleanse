What gets stripped — four stages
Stage 1 — Info dictionary
Title, Author, Subject, Keywords, Creator, Producer, Creation and Modified dates.
Stage 2 — XMP packets
Adobe's extended metadata XML format. Invisible in most viewers but readable by forensic tools.
Stage 3 — Document IDs
Unique /ID fingerprints in the PDF trailer. Survive standard cleaning — we overwrite with zeroes.
Stage 4 — PieceInfo streams
Application data blocks from InDesign, Illustrator, and Acrobat containing internal workflow metadata.
